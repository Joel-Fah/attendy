As a delegate, do more
By just doing less!

With Attendy, you can easily scan your students' QR codes to mark their attendance. It's a simple and efficient way to keep track of who's in class and who's not.

Step 1

Get your courses, students and lecturers on board.

To get started, it is crucial you have records of courses, students and lecturers of the current semester. This will help you keep track of attendance and monitor the progress of your students. So, go on and register these entities from the dashboard.

Step 2

From now on, create attendances and monitor your students.

You can start creating attendances for your courses. Students will only have to scan to mark their presence. In addition, teaching records are automatically created and attached to course attendances. Just left for you to fill in the remaining details like the lecturer's departure time...

There's more you can get out of Attendy.

Attendy is designed to make your life easier. It's a simple and efficient way to keep track of your attendance and make sure you're on top of your studies.
